Reading List for Mobile Research

Recommendation

AppTrends: A Graph-based Mobile App Recommendation System Using Usage History
Donghwan Bae, Keejun Han, Juneyoung Park, and Mun Y. Yi, in Proc. 2nd International Conference on Big Data and Smart Computing, 2015, pp. 210-216.

Personalized Mobile App Recommendation: Reconciling App Functionality and User Privacy Preference
Bin Liu, Deguang Kong, Lei Cen, Neil Zhenqiang Gong, Hongxia Jin, and Hui Xiong, in Proc. 8th International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining, 2015, pp. 315-324.

Collaborative Recommendation of Mobile Apps: A Swarm Intelligence Method
Xiao Xia, Xiaodong Wang, Xingming Zhou, and Tao Zhu, Springer on Mobile, Ubiquitous, and Intelligent Computing, vol. 274, 2014, pp. 405-412.

Functionality-Based Mobile App Recommendation by Identifying Aspects from User Reviews
Xiaoying Xu, Kaushik Dutta, and Anindya Datta, in Proc. 35th International Conference on Information Systems, 2014, pp. 1-10.

Multi-Objective Mobile App Recommendation: A System-Level Collaboration Approach
Xiao Xia, Xiaodong Wang, Jian Li, and Xingming Zhou, Computers & Electrical Engineering, vol. 40, no. 1, 2014, pp. 203-215.

Serendipitous Recommendation for Mobile Apps Using Item-Item Similarity Graph

Addressing Cold-Start in App Recommendation: Latent User Models Constructed from Twitter Followers
Jovian Lin, Kazunari Sugiyama, Min-Yen Kan, and Tat-Seng Chua, in Proc. 36th International ACM SIGIR Conference on Research and Development in Information Retrieval, 2013, pp. 283-292.

App Recommendation: A Contest between Satisfaction and Temptation
Peifeng Yin, Ping Luo, Wang-Chien Lee, and Min Wang, in Proc. 6th International Conference on Web Search and Data Mining, 2013, pp. 395-404.

Which App? A Recommender System of Applications in Markets: Implementation of the Service for Monitoring Users’ Interaction
Enrique Costa-Montenegro, Ana Belén Barragáns-Martínez, and Marta Rey-López, Expert Systems with Applications, vol. 39, no. 10, 2012, pp. 9367-9375.

Utilizing Implicit Feedback and Context to Recommend Mobile Applications from First Davidsson Use
Christoffer, and Simon Moritz, in Proc. 1st Workshop on Context-awareness in Retrieval and Recommendation, 2011, pp. 19-22.

Recommendation Algorithm of the App Store by Using Semantic Relations between Apps
Yujin Lim, Hak-Man Kim, Sanggil Kang, and Tai-hoon Kim, in Proc. 2nd International Conference on Ubiquitous Computing and Multimedia Applications, 2011, pp. 139-144.

AppJoy: Personalized Mobile Application Discovery
Bo Yan, and Guanling Chen, in Proc. 9th International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services, 2011, pp. 113-126.

A Hybrid Recommender System for Context-aware Recommendations of Mobile Applications
Wolfgang Woerndl, Christian Schueller, and Rolf Wojtech, in Proc. 23rd International Conference on Data Engineering Workshop, 2007, pp. 871-878.

Decomposable Trust for Android Applications

Efficient Privilege De-Escalation for Ad Libraries in Mobile Apps
Bin Liu, Bin Liu, Hongxia Jin, and Ramesh Govindan, in Proc. 13th Annual International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services, 2015, pp. 89-103.

Truth in Advertising: The Hidden Cost of Mobile Ads for Software Developers
Jiaping Gui, Stuart Mcilroy, Meiyappan Nagappan, and William GJ Halfond, in Proc. 37th International Conference on Software Engineering, 2015, pp. 100-110.

TaintDroid: An Information-Flow Tracking System for Realtime Privacy Monitoring on Smartphones
William Enck, Peter Gilbert, Seungyeop Han, Vasant Tendulkar, Byung-Gon Chun, Landon P. Cox, Jaeyeon Jung, Patrick McDaniel, and Anmol N. Sheth, ACM Transactions on Computer Systems, vol. 32, no. 2, 2014, pp. 5.

Dissecting Android Malware: Characterization and Evolution
Yajin Zhou, and Xuxian Jiang. in Proc. 33rd Symposium on Security and Privacy, 2012, pp. 95-109.

Static

Using Text Mining to Infer the Purpose of Permission Use in Mobile Apps
Haoyu Wang, Jason Hong, and Yao Guo, in Proc. ACM International Joint Conference on Pervasive and Ubiquitous Computing, 2015, pp. 1107-1118.

Modeling Users’ Mobile App Privacy Preferences: Restoring Usability in a Sea of Permission Settings
Jialiu Lin, Bin Liu, Norman Sadeh, and Jason I. Hong, in Proc. 10th Symposium on Usable Privacy and Security, 2014, pp. 199-212.

Checking App Behavior Against App Descriptions
Alessandra Gorla, Ilaria Tavecchia, Florian Gross, and Andreas Zeller, in Proc. 36th International Conference on Software Engineering, 2014, pp. 1025-1035.

Is this App Safe? A Large Scale Study on Application Permissions and Risk Signals

Mining Permission Request Patterns from Android and Facebook Applications

PScout: Analyzing the Android Permission Specification

in Proc. ACM Conference on Internet Measurement Conference, 2015, pp. 225-238.

A Measurement Study of Google Play

Why People Hate Your App — Making Sense of User Feedback in a Mobile App Store

Identifying Diverse Usage Behaviors of Smartphone Apps
Qiang Xu, Jeffrey Erman, Alexandre Gerber, Zhuoqing Mao, Jeffrey Pang, and Shobha Venkataraman, in Proc. ACM Conference on Internet Measurement Conference, 2011, pp. 329-344.
